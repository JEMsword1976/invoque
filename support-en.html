Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using Invoque?

You can download Invoque Free from our website. After installing it in your browser, you can start creating and managing your AI prompts and insert them into major AI websites with one click.

What's the difference between Invoque Free and Invoque Suno?

Invoque Free provides basic prompt management, supports categories, search, and one-click insert to AI sites. Invoque Suno is optimized for the SUNO music platform, allowing you to insert lyrics or styles into the right fields for a smoother music creation process.

How can I improve my AI efficiency?

With Invoque, you can organize your prompts by category and insert them with one click, saving time and boosting creativity. The Suno version lets you manage lyrics and styles separately for even more efficiency.
