Features Description

Netflix Skipper lets you automatically skip intros and credits, and auto-play the next episode for a smoother Netflix binge-watching experience.
It also offers one-click hiding of the Netflix Games section, volume adjustment with the mouse wheel, and fine-tuning of video brightness and contrast for a cleaner interface and more convenient controls. Optimize your viewing based on personal preference and ambient lighting.
Chrome Extension Link

Key Features

  • Automatically skip Netflix intros and credits
  • Auto-play next episode (if the "Next Episode" button is available)
  • Hide the Netflix Games section
  • Adjust volume with mouse wheel during playback
  • Right-click and drag to adjust video brightness and contrast
  • Supports Google account login and subscription management
  • Clean, intuitive interface with customizable features

Instructions

  1. Click the Netflix Skipper icon to open the panel and log in with your Google account.
  2. Click "Subscribe" to enjoy a 30-day free trial and toggle features as needed.
  3. Once on the Netflix website, features are enabled automatically. Right-click and drag to adjust video brightness and contrast.

Subscription & Licensing

After logging in, click the "Subscribe" button to make a payment and receive your license.
New users enjoy a 30-day free trial. Charges begin only after the trial ends, so you can continue enjoying all features.
