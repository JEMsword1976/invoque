Name Origin｜Invoque

Invoque comes from the English word "Invoke," meaning "to call upon"—just like entering a prompt to summon powerful AI responses and creativity.

We chose the spelling "Invoque" to keep the original meaning while making the name unique and recognizable, adding a touch of mystery and tech vibe.

Invoque is a professional prompt management tool. It's not just for storing and syncing your prompts, but also a bridge for communicating with AI. Every click and input is like a "summon," awakening creativity and intelligence in language models.

Product Overview

Invoque FREE is a browser extension designed for AI users to help manage and use AI prompts (PROMPT) more efficiently. This tool lets you easily organize, save, and quickly use your AI prompts, making your AI interactions more efficient and creative.

Key Features

  • Prompt Management: Create, edit, and delete prompts, categorize, filter, and search instantly.
  • Quick Search: Instantly find the prompts you need.
  • User Interface: Clean, modern design for a great experience.
  • Data Management: Local storage, import/export, real-time saving to keep your data safe.
  • Security: No user data is collected or uploaded. All data is stored locally.
  • Technical Features: Manifest V3, multi-browser support, lightweight design.
