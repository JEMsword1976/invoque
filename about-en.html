Company Introduction

Invoque was founded by an independent developer with over 30 years of experience in the IT industry. Since the 1990s, from web design and network engineering to IT consulting, we have helped many Taiwanese SMEs solve challenges in digital transformation and information management.

As technology evolves, Invoque is dedicated to app development, software solutions, and Chrome extensions, aiming to turn the latest cloud and AI technologies into practical tools that are easy for both businesses and individuals to use.

We believe IT should be a tool everyone can master. Invoque will continue to develop efficient, user-friendly, and practical products with rich industry experience and an innovative spirit, helping users improve work efficiency and embrace the digital future.

Real Cases

National Data Dashboard

A dashboard tailored for SMEs. Staff can enter business data, and the system instantly summarizes and analyzes it, helping management keep track of operations and make better decisions.

Data Visualization & Website Sync

Internal data is automatically turned into pie charts and other visuals, instantly updated on the company website, making it easy for teams and clients to see the latest info and improve communication.

Custom E-Forms

100% customized e-forms with instant calculations and dynamic options, meeting complex business needs and improving accuracy and efficiency.

Google Sheet Registration & Stats

Registration data is synced to Google Sheets in real time. Registrants can check their status, and the backend automatically counts people and payments for easy management.

Local Data Sync to Google Sheet

Local data is synced to Google Sheets, so colleagues nationwide can check progress and goals anytime, improving transparency and teamwork.

YouTube Video Downloader

Supports batch downloads, format selection (mp4/music), resolution switching, and save path settings. The intuitive interface makes it easy to get the videos or music you need.
