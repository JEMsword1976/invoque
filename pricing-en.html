FAQ

What's the difference between Invoque FREE and Invoque SUNO?

Invoque FREE is a general AI prompt management tool for all AI websites. Invoque SUNO is designed for music creation, offering exclusive lyrics and style management for Suno.

How do I install Invoque SUNO?

You can install Invoque SUNO directly from the Chrome Web Store. It's a standalone extension, separate from Invoque FREE.

Can I use both versions at the same time?

Yes, you can install both Invoque FREE and Invoque SUNO. They are independent products and can be used together.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Invoque SUNO subscription anytime in the Chrome Web Store. Service will end after the current billing period.
